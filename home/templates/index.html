About

This website is a child of two primal feelings: frustration and curiosity. Let's start with curiosity. In 2017, I began learning Python as part of my PhD research. Once I got the hang of it, I realized I enjoyed the portability, modularity, and expressiveness available within the language and began to look around and think, "Gee, I could automate that with a Python script." At the same time, I was growing tired and resentful of the amount of time I was spending on menial, repetitive tasks required for my Navy Reserve drill weekends. Since I'd been growing interested in writing scientific web applications for educational demonstrations, I figured this would be a good start to learning web app development. I hope by sharing these tools that others may be able to focus their time on more rewarding endeavors.
